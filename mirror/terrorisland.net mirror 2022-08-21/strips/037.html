Second, some of you might be curious about how things might have been with the comic. I'll give you a glimpse into one way the strip might have differed*:

9:17:36 PM factition: Oh, wait.

*Actually, the comic couldn't have been this way, because a logically necessary comic leaves no room for merely possible features. Proving this is left as an exercise to the reader.
