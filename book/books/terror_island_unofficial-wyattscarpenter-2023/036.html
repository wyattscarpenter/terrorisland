Lewis’s Notes

I’m taking over Ben’s webcomic advice column today, as a Labor Day surprise. Today, we answer a question sent in from Simon Masonicconspiracy. He asks, “Where do you get your ideas?”

That’s a good question. Most people search their hearts and minds for inspiration. Ben and I, on the other hand, being the abstract purists that we are, have decided to deduce our comic from first principles. The entirety of Terror Island, its cast, and the plotlines, has been determined on the basis of logical neccessity. I cannot recommend this approach to you, however, as that would produce the same comic Ben and I are already making.
