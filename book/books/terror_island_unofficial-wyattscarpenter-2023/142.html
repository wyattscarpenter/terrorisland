Notes, Thoughts, &c.

Ben’s Notes

Hey, it’s Gibbs and Lockley! Remember them? I guess they live in Sid and Stephen’s bathroom now. And they’re bad at handling break-ins.

To portray Omicron’s cat food in this strip, I used actual cat food. Most food photographers will tell you that this is a bad idea, since it doesn’t end up looking very realistic. But fortunately for me, I don’t care about realism.

Lewis’s Notes

While it is true that the yellow traincar is either Gibbs or Lockley, and true that the green traincar is either Gibbs or Lockley, and also true that between the two traincars, one is Gibbs and one is Lockley, it isn’t determinate in the fiction which traincar is Gibbs and which is Lockley. A prize to anyone who can use this in deriving a contradiction for this perfectly logical webcomic.
