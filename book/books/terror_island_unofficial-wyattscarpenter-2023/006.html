Lewis’s Notes

Ben usually comments on the photos or the panel composition, and I usually comment on the content of the strip, a trend that will continue today. I will give you some background. Sid and Stephen play by the traditional basic set rules for Rock-Paper-Scissors. These rules are as follows: (a) Players smack a closed fist into an open palm three times, followed by a fourth time throwing either rock, paper or scissors. (b) as players are performing the aforementioned gestures, they say “rock, paper, scissors, shoot”, punctuating each word with the gestures. (c) rock breaks scissors, scissors cut paper, paper covers rock. As you can see, the rules do not address a tie scenario.
