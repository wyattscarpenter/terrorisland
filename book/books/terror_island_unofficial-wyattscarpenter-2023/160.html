Ben’s Notes

It took Ray a little while to get into character for Omicron’s role in this strip. At first, he kept staring alertly at the doorway, as though monitoring it for suspicious activity. That’s the complete opposite of what the script required! Fortunately, he eventually pretended to be sleeping long enough for me to get some pictures.

Cats make good actors, but they require a bit more patience than gamepieces.
