Strip #204 — Monday, October 1, 2007
Stephen solved the unemployment problem by firing people.
Notes, Thoughts, &c.

Ben’s Notes

It’s Gibbs and Lockley again! They haven’t been in the comic for a while, and now you know why: Stephen fired them.

See that ceremonial sword behind Stephen in the flashback? It’s a kilt pin I wore at my friend Albert’s wedding on Saturday. I had the honor of being one of his groomsmen, which meant traditional Scottish attire — pictures will be online eventually.

Lewis’s Notes

Stephen assumes unemployment problems are a matter of the distribution of jobs among the employed, rather than an increase in the number of employed people. This puts him at both ends of the standard political continuum, and today’s strip was supposed to serve as political commentary to that effect.
