Lewis’s Notes

I think this strip represents something of a turning point, but instead of talking about that, I am going to share with you all one of my favorite jokes:

Q: What do you get if you cross the Atlantic with the Titanic?
A: About halfway.

I will say one thing about this strip. If you were to describe some of what goes on in Terror Island to someone, they could come away with the opinion that Sid and Stephen are tricksy by nature, and basically just like to scheme. I think this strip reveals that we are not dealing with two people who just like to trick each other. For Sid and Stephen, this dispute really is about the groceries.
