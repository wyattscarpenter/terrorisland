Lewis’s Notes

100. Wow. Part of me didn’t think we could sustain the strip this long. I mean, when you are brainstorming and your strip’s premise is that people don’t want to buy groceries, you imagine that you would pretty quickly run out of ways for them to fail to purchase groceries. But we have kept it up, no groceries seem to have been purchased, at least, not by our protagonists, and the strip is coming along quite well.
