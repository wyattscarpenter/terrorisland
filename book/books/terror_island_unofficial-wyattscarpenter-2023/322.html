Ben’s Notes

Another aspect of the second incompleteness theorem that needs to be emphasized is that it does not show that S can only be proved consistent in a system that is stronger than S. To say that S is consistent is not to endorse the axioms of S, and a proof that S is consistent need not use the axioms of S itself.
