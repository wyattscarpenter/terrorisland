Lewis’s Notes

Today I bring you fan-news and a crazy Ben anecdote.

First, we’ve been adoringly ripped off. If you are curious about the punchline of that fan strip, I direct you to an explanation. And, yes, if you check those credits, they make it clear that I was director and Ben was merely codirector.

And now the anecdote. One time (October 7, 2004), Ben had introduced me to wikipedia. And I had never come across the word “neologism”, which gets used a lot on wikipedia when people want to delete articles. So I send Ben an IM (he lived on the same floor as me at college, but in a different suite).

(18:56:11)ultralewis:what is neologism?
(18:56:19)factition:Neologism?
(18:56:28)factition:Lewis, that’s a word you just made up.

Then he runs to my room, to see my reaction and make sure I got the joke. At which point I say, “Ben, how long have you been waiting to use that one?” To which he replied “two years”.

And I believe him.
