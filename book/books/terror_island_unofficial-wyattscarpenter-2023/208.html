Ben’s Notes

Ralph Nostradamus writes, “Are those really photographs in your comics? They look more like drawings to me. Childish drawings made with crayons.”

Yes, Ralph, this is a photocomic. Photocomics have a long tradition, dating back to the early days of either comics or photography, whichever came first. Notable photocartoonists include such people as Lewis Powell and Ben Heaton. In conclusion, thanks for writing in.
