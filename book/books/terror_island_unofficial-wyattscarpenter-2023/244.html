Ben’s Notes

Fun fact: while trying to get pictures of Jame skateboarding down the ramp, I almost lost the piece that plays him. On one of the takes, it missed the bowl I’d set at the bottom and went into a crack between two boards. Fortunately, it didn’t go in very deep, so I got it out with some tweezers.

If that had failed, I would have had to get out another blue tiddlywink. I have a lot of backups for Jame, but have never yet needed to use any of them. Now that he doesn’t have a stunt double, the understudies may be more likely to see eventual use.
