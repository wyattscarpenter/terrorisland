Ben’s Notes

It seems Tinyfolk‘s national tour is taking him to California this week. I’m looking forward to seeing him play in San Francisco tomorrow, and recommend that those of you who live around there go check it out.

I found out about Tinyfolk a few years ago, when he wrote a song about me called “An Ode to Ben Heaton.” I’ve still never met him in person, though. Perhaps that will change! Expect some sort of comments about this on Wednesday, unless I forget to go or something.

Lewis’s Notes

Earlier today I drove past a car place called “JUST BRAKES and more.” All day long I have been thinking about that place, and how absolutely awful of a name that is. Since I’ve been thinking about it all day though, maybe it is a cleverly horrid name, in that it certainly embedded itself in my mind pretty thoroughly.
