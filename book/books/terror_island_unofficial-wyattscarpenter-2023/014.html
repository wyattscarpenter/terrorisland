Lewis’s Notes

While Ben is busy showing he’s not slave to the four panel format, I am going to humbly supplicate you, our readers, for a favor. I’ve decided that Terror Island requires, well, more exposure. And one sure way to get exposure is Internet drama. That being said, if you have a web comic, or know someone who does, and want to stage an entertaining internet fight with us, we are open to the idea. Just so long as it has a decent chance of getting noticed by Websnark or Comixpedia or what have you.

Also, it would be optimal if you or your friend wanted to be the ‘bad guy’ in the fight. Ben and I want to maintain our reputations as the nice guys of gamepiece photocomics.
