Notes, Thoughts, &c.

Ben’s Notes

Comic-Con just ended last night, and now I’m heading off to Michigan for a couple weeks. My Internet access will be sporadic there, but I should be able to get content to Lewis in such a way that our update schedule will be unaffected.

Lewis’s Notes

Since we had a couple strips done before Comic-Con, we are living in the world of cramped speech bubbles again, but only briefly.

If you are coming here from the Fleen link, there will hopefully be something on that front later today, technology willing. Certainly it will happen early this week. If you aren’t coming from the Fleen link, feel free to ignore that.

I’m also going to briefly plug another site of mine here, which potentially no one but me will be interested in. I had been reading webcomics blogs and found myself filled with a desire to post long comments. Rather than be all talky on someone else’s webspace, I decided that I would just set up a blog on my webspace, so that later, when I want to see the things I wrote, they’re all in one place. For instance, instead of posting about Comic-Con here, I’m going to post thoughts and stuff on that site. I will say this though, Comic-Con was a lot of fun, and I hope I’m able to go again next year.

In strip news, Ben and I plotted out Terror Island for a good month of strips on the drive back from San Diego, and we have a couple of plotlines that we know are coming after that. Which was a lot of fun to plan out, and will hopefully help keep this fun for us, rather than stressful or what have you.
