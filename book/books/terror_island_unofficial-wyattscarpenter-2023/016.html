Lewis’s Notes

We moved, but in a way where spatial metaphors break down, since you all found us in the same place.

Put more coherently; we aquired the domain, terrorisland.net, which currently works alongside waxintellectual.com. We may eventually break up that dual functionality, but for now, they both work. This was done partially at the behest of people who mentioned that our domain and comic title should relate. (Tell that to Daily Dinosaur Comics or Order of the Stick, I say)

Today, Ben and I are en route to San Diego for Comic-Con, which is exciting and fun. If you see us there, we will likely try and trick you into promoting our comic. This constitutes fair warning under the Stockholm guidelines.
