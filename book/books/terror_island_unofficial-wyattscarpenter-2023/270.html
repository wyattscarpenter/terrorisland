Ben’s Notes

Today’s Ronald O’keeffe and the Ricotta Cove Irregulars has a good cameo from a Terror Island character. Go check it out.

One of the things I like about Ronald O’keeffe and the Ricotta Cove Irregulars is that they’ve been doing set construction the right way from the start. The strips pretty much all take place in a single restaurant, with a consistent, well-organized setup to it. That sort of passive sense of place is something our comic doesn’t manage all that often. (I’m not really sure where today’s strip takes place, for example.)
