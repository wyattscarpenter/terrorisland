Strip #131 — Friday, April 13, 2007
The Green Grocer can never pass up an opportunity to announce himself.
Notes, Thoughts, &c.

Ben’s Notes

Fran Seabane writes in, asking, “Why doesn’t your webcomic update every day? I want to see more than three strips a week!”

I’m glad to know you want more Terror Island, Fran! But the reason we only update on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays is that our traffic data shows that more people visit the site on those days. Admittedly, I may have the direction of causation backwards there, but there is definitely a causal link.

Lewis’s Notes

You can tell that The Green Grocer is fairly brave, since he was willing to fight The Obvious Dentist even though The Obvious Dentist has the advantage of having actual super powers. I would suggest that the henchmen balance that out, but the odds are that they wouldn’t participate in the fight, so much as give insightful analyses of the quips traded between the combatants.
