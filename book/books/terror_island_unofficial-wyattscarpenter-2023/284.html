Lewis’s Notes

It is important to remember that Sid and Stephen aren’t even super heroes, so they get a x8 multiplier to their scores for defeating a council of supervillains. Defeating a council of supervillains is normally adds half again as many points to one’s score as one already had, however, sadly, the recursive rules for scorekeeping don’t include a base case. While we know that their scores are now (x + 8*(x/2)) where x is their previous score, there is simply no fact of the matter as to what their current scores are.
